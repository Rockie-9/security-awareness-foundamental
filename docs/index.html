Security as Professional Responsibility

In the EU context, security is not about blind compliance.
It is about professional judgment and duty of care.

Authorized
You are technically allowed to access something.
Appropriate
You have a clear business need to use it.
Emphasize: authorization does not equal appropriateness. Professionals are expected to think, not just follow checklists.

Traceability ‚â† Surveillance

Logging and monitoring exist to protect professionals, ensure accountability, and prevent negligence.

  • Logs are reviewed only when incidents occur
  • No continuous individual monitoring
  • Aligned with GDPR legitimate interest & proportionality
Use precise language: proportional, legitimate interest, duty of care. Avoid words like "tracking" or "watching people".

Good Intentions Do Not Prevent Liability

In the U.S. legal environment, intent is not a defense.

Intent

Legal exposure is based on what happened.
Stress litigation reality without sounding threatening. Focus on predictability of consequences.
